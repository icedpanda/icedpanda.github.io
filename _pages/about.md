Hello! I am Zhangchi Qiu, a Research Fellow at Griffith University.
My research focuses on artificial intelligence and data mining, with particular interests in large language models, knowledge graphs, and recommender systems. I am especially interested in developing knowledge-aware and trustworthy AI systems for real-world applications.
I am a core member of the [AI4Health Lab](https://ai4healthlab.github.io/index.html), led by Prof. [Alan Wee-Chung Lew](https://experts.griffith.edu.au/7401-alan-weechung-liew), and the [TrustAGI Lab](https://trust-agi.github.io), led by Prof. [Shirui Pan](https://shiruipan.github.io).
Previously, I received my PhD in Computer Science from Griffith University, where I was advised by Prof. Alan Wee-Chung Lew, Prof. Shirui Pan, and Assoc. Prof. [Hui Tian](https://experts.griffith.edu.au/8285-hui-tian).
